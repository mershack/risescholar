Recruitment Innovation for Student Empowerment (R.I.S.E) is a non-profit consulting group committed to providing students preparing for college education with professional guidance; to make informed decisions about their career path.

R.I.S.E. partners with academia, families, communities, corporations, and institutions in seeking financial support and leveraging available resources towards a R.I.S.E. scholar's college education. R.I.S.E. collaborates with students, faculty, and staff to identify obstacles, provide early intervention and/or services to empower students in attaining their educational goals.
